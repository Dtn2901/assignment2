TFT

Teamfight Tactics (TFT) is an auto-battler game developed by Riot Games, set in the League of Legends universe. In TFT, players compete against seven opponents in strategic, turn-based combat. The game revolves around assembling a team of champions, each with unique abilities and traits, to battle on an evolving battlefield. Players purchase, position, and upgrade their champions throughout the game rounds, aiming to outmaneuver and outlast their opponents. TFT combines elements of strategy, resource management, and adaptability, as players must carefully plan their compositions, itemize their champions, and adjust their tactics based on the ever-changing battlefield conditions. With regular updates, a vibrant community, and a dynamic meta, TFT offers a challenging and rewarding experience for players seeking strategic depth and competitive gameplay within the League of Legends universe.
