About Me

Hi! I'm Darren, I'm currently enrolled in Coder Academy and hope to become a Web Developer in the future. Over the past 5 years I've worked numerous jobs ranging from Hospitality, Warehousing and Banking. This year I've decided to take a leap and dive into the world of coding.

Growing up, I've always wanted to learn more about how everything works on a computer whether it be games, a site, or a program. So i dove into my first IT class in year 9 based on Game Development. Having completed Year 12, I've decided to not do any further studies but instead work to gain skills and an understanding of what i want to do in the future. With Great Leadership and Organizational Skills, I believe I can excel at Coder Academy. These skills have been honed through my diverse work experiences, where I've had to effectively manage teams, prioritize tasks, and navigate complex situations. In the fast-paced environment of web development, these abilities will enable me to coordinate projects efficiently, communicate effectively with teammates, and adapt to the evolving demands of the industry.
